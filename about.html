About Doortronix

Over two decades of automatic door expertise in the New York tri-state area.

Our Story

Doortronix has been serving the NY tri-state area since 2001 — selling, installing, and servicing automatic door systems for hospitals, retailers, hotels, government buildings, and commercial properties from Manhattan to the Connecticut shore.

We are the NY Sales & Service Division of Doortronix — the same company that manufactures the Revolution system in Malakoff, Texas. That factory relationship gives our customers access to factory-direct pricing, technical support direct from the engineers who built the door, and the fastest parts availability in the region.

Beyond Doortronix products, our technicians are trained and experienced on every major brand in the field — Stanley, Horton, Besam, NABCO, Record, dorma/kaba, and more. If it opens automatically, we can install it, maintain it, and fix it when it breaks.
