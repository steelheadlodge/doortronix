The Door That Ended "Door Off Track" Service Calls

As the Doortronix NY Sales & Service Division, we install and support the Doortronix Revolution DT-3000 — the only automatic sliding door built on a rod-and-bearing drive instead of a track-and-roller system.

  • No track means no panels jumping, no rollers wearing out
  • I-beam header — zero deflection, stays aligned for life
  • Magnetic breakaway sidelights — no ball catches to fail
  • Self-diagnosing controller — LED fault codes, no programmer needed
  • 5-year warranty on the rod and bearing — backed by the factory
