Preventive Maintenance Contracts

The lowest-cost service call is the one you never have to make. A PM contract keeps your doors safe, compliant, and out of the emergency call rotation.

Quarterly PM

Full inspection and lubrication every 90 days. Recommended for high-traffic entrances at hospitals, grocery stores, and transit hubs. Includes written inspection report.

Semi-Annual PM

Two visits per year covering all mechanical and electrical components. Appropriate for moderate-traffic commercial and retail entrances.

Annual PM

Annual inspection combined with ANSI/BHMA compliance test. Ideal for office buildings, hospitality, and government facilities with lower cycle counts.

What's Included

All PM visits include: sensor alignment and calibration, force and speed testing, hardware inspection, motor and drive check, full written report, and priority scheduling for repairs.

Code & Compliance Inspections

Many facilities are required to have their automatic doors inspected and documented annually. We provide the written report you need to satisfy your facilities management, insurance, or regulatory requirements.

ANSI/BHMA A156.10

Standard for power-operated pedestrian doors. We test opening force, closing speed, sensor detection zones, and breakout force — and document each measurement against the standard.

ADA / Section 504 Compliance

Verify that activation devices, clear width, opening force, and timing meet ADA and local accessibility code requirements. Written report provided for your records.

Joint Commission Readiness

Healthcare facilities undergoing Joint Commission surveys need documentation of door functionality and safety device operation. We know what surveyors look for.

Annual Written Report

Every inspection includes a signed written report identifying all deficiencies, recommended corrections, and confirmation of items in compliance — suitable for your facilities file.

Modernization & Upgrades

You don't always need to replace the whole door. We can often upgrade existing systems with new operators, sensors, or activation devices at a fraction of new-door cost.

Operator Replacement

Replace a worn or obsolete drive mechanism while keeping the existing frame and panels. Often the most cost-effective path for doors with good glass and framing.

Touchless Activation

Upgrade from push plates to wave-to-open sensors or presence detectors. Popular in post-COVID retrofits for healthcare, grocery, and high-touch environments.

Access Control Integration

Integrate your automatic door with card readers, intercom systems, or building management systems. We coordinate with your security integrator or handle the full scope.

Breakaway Upgrade

Add or retrofit magnetic breakaway sidelights for emergency egress compliance. Required in many jurisdictions — we install and certify the breakout force per ANSI A156.10.
