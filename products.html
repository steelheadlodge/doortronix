Doortronix Division Our #1 Recommendation

Doortronix Revolution — DT-3000

The system that ended the biggest service call in the industry. The Revolution runs on a 1" chrome steel rod with a precision linear motion bearing — no track, no rollers, nothing to jump, nothing to fill with grit and stop working.

As the NY division of Doortronix, we install and service more Revolution doors than anyone else in the region. We know this system inside and out.

  • Bi-parting and single-slide configurations
  • FBO (Full Breakout) and FSL (Sidelite) emergency egress
  • I-beam header — zero deflection under building load
  • Self-diagnosing controller, no programmer required
  • Magnetic breakaway sidelights — no ball catches
  • 5-year warranty on rod and bearing
  • Widths from 4' to 20'+, custom-manufactured to order
